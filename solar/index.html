Withdraw to your Bank

How does this work?

Each solar panel is an independent arrangement. Individuals browse and participate in crowdfunds to provide the neccessary funds for a verified builder to deploy.

An tamperproof IoT device is attached to the solar panel to track and publish the real-time data of energy production.

The NestEgg dashboard tracks your shares of ownership and delivers your share of utility. As your panels generate energy, it will be sold for euros that can be withdrawn to your bank account.
